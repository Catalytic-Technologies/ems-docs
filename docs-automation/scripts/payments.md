Welcome to the EMS Payments guide. This tutorial shows you how to record a fee payment when a guardian pays at school.

Go to Finance in the left sidebar, then click Payments. Click the New Payment button to open the payment form.

In the Student field, search for the student by name or admission number. Click the student's name when it appears in the dropdown. The system will load their outstanding invoices automatically.

Enter the amount paid by the guardian. Select the payment method — this can be Cash, Bank Transfer, Mobile Money, or Cheque. Enter a reference number if available, such as a bank deposit slip number.

The system will automatically allocate the payment to the student's oldest unpaid invoice. If you need to change which invoice receives the payment, you can adjust the allocation manually.

Click Save to record the payment. A receipt is generated automatically with a unique receipt number. You can print the receipt or download it as a PDF to give to the guardian.

If your school is set up with SchoolPay, payments made by guardians through mobile money are imported automatically. You do not need to enter those manually.

That is how you record a fee payment in EMS. Always save the receipt for the guardian as proof of payment.
