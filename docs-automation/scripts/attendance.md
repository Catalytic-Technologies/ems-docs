Welcome to the EMS Attendance module. This guide shows you how to mark attendance for your class.

Start by clicking Attendance in the left sidebar. You will see a list of your assigned classes. Click on the class you want to mark attendance for.

The system loads your full student list for today's date automatically. If you need to mark attendance for a different date, use the date picker at the top of the page.

For each student, you can mark them as Present, Absent, Late, or Excused. Click the appropriate button next to each student's name.

If most students are present, use the Mark All Present button at the top to mark everyone present at once. You can then click Absent or Late for individual students who are not present. This saves you a lot of time.

You can also add a note for any absent student by clicking the note icon next to their name. This is useful for recording the reason for absence.

When you have finished marking all students, click the Save Attendance button at the bottom of the page. The record is saved immediately and is visible to the school administrator.

That is all you need to mark attendance. Remember to mark attendance every day for your class. Thank you for watching.
