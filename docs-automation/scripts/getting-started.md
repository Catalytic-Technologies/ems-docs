Welcome to EMS, the School Operating System. This short guide will help you log in and find your way around the system for the first time.

Open your web browser and go to the EMS web address provided by your school administrator. You will see the login page.

Enter your email address and your password, then click Sign In. If this is your first time logging in, use the temporary password from your welcome email. You will be asked to set a new password immediately.

After signing in, you will be taken to your Dashboard. The dashboard shows the most important information for your role at a glance. A school administrator will see school-wide statistics, a teacher will see their classes and upcoming tasks, and a bursar will see the financial summary.

The left sidebar is your main navigation. Click any section to go to that module. Items with a small arrow can be expanded to show sub-pages.

The top navigation bar has the school name, a search button, and your profile icon in the top right corner. Click your profile icon to access your account settings or to log out.

If you get stuck, use the Help button on any page, or click the chat button in the bottom right corner to ask our support assistant a question.

Welcome to EMS. We hope you find the system easy to use.
