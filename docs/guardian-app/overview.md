# Guardian App Overview

<span className="role-badge role-badge--guardian">Guardian</span>

The Guardian App gives parents and legal guardians real-time access to their child's school information. It is available as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** accessible from any browser, and as a **native mobile app** for Android and iOS.

## What Guardians Can Do

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Fee Statements** | View current invoice and payment history |
| **Make Payments** | Pay fees directly via mobile money |
| **Attendance** | View child's attendance record and alerts |
| **Report Cards** | Download end-of-term report cards |
| **Notifications** | Receive school announcements and alerts |
| **Messages** | Communicate with the school |
| **Calendar** | View school events and term dates |

## Access Methods

### PWA (Browser)
Guardians can access the app at:
```
https://guardian.ems.school
```
They can also **install it on their home screen** for an app-like experience without downloading from a store.

### Mobile App
Available on:
- **Android** — Google Play Store (search "EMS Guardian")
- **iOS** — Apple App Store (search "EMS Guardian")

## Linking a Guardian to a Student

Guardian accounts are automatically created and linked to students when:
- A student is admitted through the admissions portal (guardian contact from the application)
- A School Admin manually adds the guardian's details to a student's profile

The guardian receives a welcome email with their login credentials.
