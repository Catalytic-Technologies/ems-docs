# Guardian Progressive Web App (PWA) Guide

<span className="role-badge role-badge--guardian">Guardian</span>

The Guardian PWA works in any modern browser on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No app store download is needed.

## Accessing the PWA

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge).
2. Go to: `https://guardian.ems.school`
3. Log in with your email and password.

## Installing to Your Home Screen

Installing the PWA gives you a dedicated app icon and a full-screen experience (no browser address bar).

### On Android (Chrome):
1. Open the PWA in Chrome.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top right.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**.
4. Confirm the name and tap **Add**.

### On iPhone / iPad (Safari):
1. Open the PWA in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.

The EMS Guardian app icon now appears on your home screen.

## Navigating the PWA

After logging in, the home screen shows your child's summary. If you have multiple children enrolled in the same school, use the **child selector** at the top to switch between them.

### Bottom Navigation Bar

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **Home** | Quick summary — today's attendance, balance due |
| **Finance** | Invoice and payment history |
| **Academic** | Attendance, report cards |
| **Messages** | School communications |
| **Profile** | Account settings |

## Viewing Fee Statements

1. Tap **Finance**.
2. Your current invoice is shown with the balance due.
3. Scroll down to see previous terms' payment history.
4. Tap any payment to see the receipt.

## Downloading a Report Card

1. Tap **Academic**.
2. Tap **Report Cards**.
3. Select the term.
4. Tap **Download** to save the PDF report card to your device.
