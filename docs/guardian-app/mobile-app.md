# Guardian Mobile App

<span className="role-badge role-badge--guardian">Guardian</span>

The EMS Guardian mobile app is a native Android and iOS app for parents. It offers the same features as the PWA with added benefits like **push notifications** and **offline viewing** of previously loaded data.

## Downloading the App

- **Android**: Search "EMS Guardian" on the **Google Play Store** and tap Install.
- **iOS**: Search "EMS Guardian" on the **Apple App Store** and tap Get.

## Setting Up the App

1. Open the app after installation.
2. On the welcome screen, enter your **School Code** (provided by your school — found in the school's communication to parents).
3. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
4. Tap **Sign In**.

:::tip First time logging in?
Use the temporary password sent to your email by the school. You will be prompted to set a new password on first login.
:::

## Push Notifications

The mobile app supports push notifications for:
- New messages from the school
- Fee payment reminders
- Attendance alerts (if your child is marked absent)
- New report card available

Go to your phone's **Settings → Notifications → EMS Guardian** to manage notification preferences.

## Offline Access

Data loaded while connected is available offline for viewing. Payments and messages require an internet connection.

## Troubleshooting the App

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| Cannot log in | Check your email and password; use **Forgot Password** on the login screen |
| Not receiving notifications | Check notification permissions in your phone settings |
| App shows outdated data | Pull down on the screen to refresh |
| School not found with school code | Contact your school admin to confirm the correct code |
