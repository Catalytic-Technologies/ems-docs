# Staff Management

<span className="role-badge role-badge--admin">School Admin</span>

The Staff module manages all staff user accounts — creating accounts, assigning roles, and managing teacher profiles used in timetabling.

## Viewing Staff

Go to **Administration → Staff** to see all staff accounts.

## Adding a Staff Member

1. Click **Add Staff Member**.
2. Fill in:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Staff member's full name |
| **Email** | Used as their login username |
| **Role** | School Admin / Bursar / Teacher |
| **Phone** | Contact number |
| **Staff ID** | Internal staff number (optional) |
| **Subject Specialisation** | Subjects this teacher is qualified to teach |

3. Click **Save** — the staff member receives a welcome email with a temporary password.

## Editing a Staff Member

Click a staff member's name to open their profile, then click **Edit**.

## Deactivating an Account

To remove access without deleting the record:
1. Open the staff profile.
2. Click **Deactivate**.

Deactivated users cannot log in but their historical records (attendance marked, assessments entered) are preserved.

## Teacher Profiles

Teacher profiles extend staff accounts with timetabling information:
- **Maximum periods per day/week** — used by the timetable builder to prevent overloading
- **Available days** — for part-time teachers
- **Subjects taught**

Manage teacher profiles from **Academic → Timetables → Teacher Profiles** or from the staff member's profile page.
