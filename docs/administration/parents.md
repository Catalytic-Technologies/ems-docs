# Parents & Guardians

<span className="role-badge role-badge--admin">School Admin</span>

The Parents module manages guardian accounts — the parents and legal guardians linked to enrolled students. Guardian accounts provide access to the Guardian App.

## Viewing Guardians

Go to **Administration → Parents** to see all guardian accounts.

## Guardian Accounts

Guardian accounts are automatically created when:
- A student is admitted through the Admissions module (guardian contact details from the application)
- A student is manually added with guardian details

Each guardian account is linked to one or more students. A guardian can have multiple children in the system.

## Sending a Temporary Password

If a guardian cannot access the app, you can reset their password:
1. Find the guardian in the list.
2. Click their name to open the profile.
3. Click **Send Temporary Password**.
4. A new temporary password is emailed to the guardian's registered email address.

:::tip
Ask guardians to change their temporary password on first login for security.
:::

## Editing Guardian Details

1. Open the guardian's profile.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Update the details and click **Save**.

## Guardian Portal Access

Guardians access the system through:
- **Guardian PWA** — accessible at `https://guardian.ems.school` from any browser
- **Guardian Mobile App** — Android and iOS

See the [Guardian App guide →](../guardian-app/overview) for what guardians can do.
