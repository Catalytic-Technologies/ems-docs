# Payment Plans

<span className="role-badge role-badge--admin">School Admin</span> <span className="role-badge role-badge--bursar">Bursar</span>

Payment plans allow guardians to pay fees in instalments rather than a lump sum, with defined payment milestones and due dates.

## Creating a Payment Plan

1. Go to **Finance → Payment Plans**.
2. Click **New Payment Plan**.
3. Select the **student** and the **invoice** the plan applies to.
4. Define the instalments:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Instalment Name** | e.g. "First Instalment", "Balance" |
| **Amount** | Amount due for this instalment |
| **Due Date** | When this instalment is due |

5. Add as many instalments as needed. The total must equal the invoice amount.
6. Click **Save**.

## Monitoring Payment Plans

The payment plans list shows:
- Which students have active plans
- Which instalments are upcoming or overdue
- Payment progress per plan

## Recording Payments Against a Plan

Payments are still recorded through the standard [Payments](./payments) screen. EMS automatically allocates payments to the current instalment of the plan.
